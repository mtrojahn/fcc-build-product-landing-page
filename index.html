How it Works? Devs Coffes

 

Simulation of Devs Working

 

Here at Dev-O-Matic you can provide coffee for a developer and have him working for you for the day.
Better quality coffee will lure better developers!

Junior Dev

Can't even tell good coffee from dirty water. Will do any kind of job as long he add it to his Github portifolio.

Middle Dev

Tasted the good coffee a few times and now wants it real bad. He will give you good results but you will be missing a little of TDD or DDD.

Senior Dev

No coffe you provide is ever good enough but if you try to bond giving him some custom brewed beers he will love you.
